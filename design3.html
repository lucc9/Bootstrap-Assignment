Darth Vader: Gifted Engineer?

An assignment in which we were tasked with arguing against the notion that Darth Vader was a skilled engineer, using a medium besides our own voices.

The video was presented at a debate held in association with ESB, Dell Ireland and AHEAD.

I mostly worked on research, writing and the rebuttal.
